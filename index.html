This lesson application was designed for the purpose of teaching Odd Numbers and Even Numbers to the kids at Little People Kindergarten School.

Objectives

  1. Identify odd and even numbers from 1 to 10.
  2. Understand the concept of pairs and how they relate to even numbers.
  3. Develop listening skills through sound recognition of odd and even numbers.
  4. Encourage interaction through a fun and engaging game.

Key Language

  1. Odd
  2. Even
  3. Pair
  4. Number
  5. Count
